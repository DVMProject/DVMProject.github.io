DVMProject

The DVMProject is a collection of repositories that make up a Digital Voice Modem implementation. See our GitHub repository with the link below. Originally a fork of the MMDVM (https://github.com/g4klx/MMDVM) project.

Please feel free to reach out to us for help, comments or otherwise, on our Discord: https://discord.gg/3pBe8xgrEz
